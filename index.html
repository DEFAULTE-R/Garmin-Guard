Critical Infrastructure Vulnerabilities

Rural healthcare facilities in developing regions face significant challenges in maintaining reliable cold chain storage for vaccines and medicines. Power fluctuations, equipment failures, and lack of monitoring systems lead to preventable losses.

Temperature Excursions

Undetected temperature fluctuations can render vaccines ineffective within hours.

Equipment Failures

Refrigeration units often fail without warning, leading to catastrophic losses.

Limited Connectivity

Remote locations lack reliable internet for cloud-based monitoring solutions.

Offline-First Design

Stores data locally and syncs when connectivity is available. Works entirely offline when needed. [design]

How It Works

  1. Current sensors monitor power consumption patterns of refrigeration units
  2. Edge device analyzes patterns using trained ML models to detect anomalies
  3. Local alerts are triggered for maintenance staff via buzzer/LED
  4. Data is stored locally and transmitted when connectivity allows
  5. Central dashboard provides overview for district health officers
